Anxiety

Symptoms

trouble concentrating or making decisions. feeling irritable, tense or restless. experiencing nausea or abdominal distress. having heart palpitations. sweating, trembling or shaking. trouble sleeping. having a sense of impending danger, panic or doom

Causes

childhood trauma, social isolation, negative life events, stress relating to work or education, physical or mental health problems, and social and societal pressures.

Solutions

Cognitive Behaviourl Therapy, exercise, psychotherapy, medication, correct breathing techniques, lifestyle adjustments, relaxation techniques
